About My Website

My website is going to be called HaveAGoodTrip.co. This is a business concept that my two friends (Max and Tata) and I have been discussing about for the last year. Now, with the opportunity to create my own website in Web Technologies, this concept might finally come into fruition. Our website will consist of various aspects relating to fashion and lifestyle where we both sell and research a curated list of products, brands and companies that we all represent, resonate with and express interest in. Since it's a lifestyle brand, we also want to display the facets of our lives that we really enjoy such as skateboarding, travel & photography, art, and music.

Objective

The objective of my website is to promote the culture of fashion to those who express similar interests, while also showcasing the different hobbies and passions that my friends and I have in common. The main purpose of creating this website is so that we can envision what the interface would be like if we were to launch our brand, plus it will provide us with insights as to how we should market products and provide the best user experience for our potential customers.

Target Audience

HaveAGoodTrip.co aims to target 18-25 year old individuals who are interested in shifting their lifestyle via fashion. Being people who aspire to achieve more in our daily lives, We want to provide inspirations and share resources to motivate those who struggle to execute their ideas in life. By targeting people from the ages of 18-25 we hope to generate a trend and precedent of what collaborative work can take us. Through our marketing strategies, we urge to form a community around like-minded people who seek to change their lifestyles for the better.

Content

The four pages of content will include: product offering, reviews, camera roll, and used grails. Firstly we have the product offering page which will be our main shop wherre we list the products that our brand offers. Although we currently have no products, the inferface of this page will display photos of products that have similar designs and styles to our concepts. The reviews page will be a blog-style page which will feature our favorite brands and their products as well as an in depth review. The camera roll page will consist of photos and videos, both from ourselves and third party users, to showcase the lifestyle that we resonate with as well as provide resources such as travel destinations, cool stores to visit, best restaurants in town, and much more. Finally, we have the used grails page which is a second hand market type page where we resell any used clothing that we can obtain that fits embodies our brand image.
